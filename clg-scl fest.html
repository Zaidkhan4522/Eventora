FAQs

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Everything you need to know before booking your campus fest with us.

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 4–6 weeks in advance for smaller events, and 2–3 months ahead for large multi-day fests. Early booking ensures better artist availability and pricing.

Can you manage events on college campuses with restricted entry?

Absolutely. We regularly work within campus guidelines, liaise with security departments, and ensure all our crew members carry proper ID and documentation for entry clearance.

Do you handle celebrity artist bookings?

Yes. We have direct relationships with talent agencies and can book artists across music, comedy, and motivation speaking. Artist fees are presented transparently with no hidden commission mark-up.

What if there is a last-minute change or emergency on the fest day?

We always have contingency plans in place — backup power, standby equipment, and an on-ground team lead who can make real-time decisions. Our 24/7 ops line is active throughout the event.

Can the packages be customised?

Every package is fully customisable. We treat published packages as starting points and build a bespoke plan around your specific needs, student strength, and venue constraints.
