FAQs

Questions Before You Book?

We've answered the most common birthday planning questions right here.

How early should I book my child's birthday party?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for smaller parties and 4–6 weeks for larger or milestone events. Popular dates like weekends and school holidays book up fast, so early booking is always best.

Can I customise a package with specific services?

Absolutely. All our packages are fully customisable. You can add, remove, or swap individual services to build exactly the birthday experience you want within your budget.

Do you manage birthdays at home or only at venues?

We manage birthdays anywhere — your home, a rented hall, a garden, a rooftop, a restaurant, or a resort. Our team assesses the space and adapts the setup accordingly.

What happens if there's a last-minute change in guest count?

We plan for flexibility. Inform us at least 48 hours before the event for catering adjustments. For décor or seating, we can accommodate minor changes even on the day of setup.

Can you arrange surprise parties without the birthday person finding out?

Yes — this is one of our specialties! We coordinate secretly with the organiser, use discreet vendor communication, and time the entire setup to ensure the surprise is perfectly preserved until the big reveal moment.
